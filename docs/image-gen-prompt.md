# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include fill-height tables, pitch maps, glass dimensions, tray dimensions, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished glass harp setup: a carefully arranged set of tuned wine glasses on a spill-safe dark wooden tray with subtle water levels visible, removable moisture-safe glass ID tags placed low and away from the rims, a small water vessel and soft towel integrated into the staging, elegant but practical spacing for wet-finger rim playing, clear glass reflections, realistic tabletop scale, clean neutral studio background, soft directional studio lighting, ~50mm lens, high detail, no readable labels.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic scene of the glass harp being played in a quiet recital room or workshop, natural light through the glass set, a performer circling a wetted finger around one rim while other glasses sit securely on a damped tray, water pipette, towel, and measurement notebook blurred nearby, environmental storytelling about measured fill workflow and spill control, realistic hands, delicate reflections, shallow depth of field, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the signature glass-harp mechanism: a wetted fingertip circling the thin rim of a clear wine glass with a visible water meniscus inside, neighboring glasses softly blurred, a stable pad or tray surface below, tiny water droplets and rim reflections, soft raking light, high detail, no fill marks or pitch callouts.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, readable note labels, dimension callouts, impossible identical pitch claims, broken glass, spilling water onto electronics, extra fingers, or turning the setup into a rotating glass armonica.
